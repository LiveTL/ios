Get live translations for YouTube streams, crowdsourced from bilingual viewers!
Have you ever wanted live translations for HoloLive/Vtuber streams? Well, look no further than LiveTL!

LiveTL (Live TransLate) is an app that separates translations from regular chat messages in YouTube streams. It includes the following features:
* Filter translations for your language (messages tagged with [en], es -, etc.)
* Select your favorite translators
* Block spammers polluting translations
* Clean interface
* Archived stream (VOD) support
* Custom user/mod filters
* Dark mode
* Plus much, much more!
